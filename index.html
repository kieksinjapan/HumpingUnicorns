WHAT ARE HUMPING UNICORNS?

Launched in July 2023, Humping Unicorns are a fun NFT collection that live on the Avalanche Network. They are randomly created with various traits and attributes, some common, some rare.

We hope there is a humper out there for everyone, whether you are feeling cheeky, cute, angry or just not satisfied... Humping Unicorns will allow you to express your emotions whatever they may be!

WHAT IS HUMP CLUB?

Hump Club is our exclusive club for those who hold one of our super rare unicorns.
We host bi-weekly giveaways and our members are prioritised for WL's, collabs and future fun.

How do I join?
If you were lucky enough to mint a 1/1, a rare unicorn, or have accquired a custom through a burn session, you would have been granted access.
